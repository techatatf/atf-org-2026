Who We Are

Three decades at the
forefront of African
technology.

The African Technology Forum is a pan-African institution dedicated to harnessing technology for Africa's development challenges. Since 1987, we have built one of the continent's most trusted networks of technology practitioners, policymakers, and innovators.

We work at the intersection of consulting, innovation, and capacity building — creating systemic change across sectors and borders.
